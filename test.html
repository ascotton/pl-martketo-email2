Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Therapy Essentials Platform?

It is a proprietary tool that PresenceLearning created for our own Clinicians to use in remote therapy almost a decade ago. We are confident in the platform’s ability to deliver therapy and have made a version of it available to you. The platform includes:
  • An online therapy room that has secure video conferencing, interactive tools such as a whiteboard, word processor, fun games and therapy-based activities
  • On-demand training modules to help you get comfortable with the platform and how to use activities in therapy
  • Chat-based support to assist if you have any questions
  • Some licenses give you access to a large library of digitized academic and cognitive assessments.

Which specialties can use this Platform?

The Therapy Essentials platform serves multi-disciplinary teams focused on k-12 ages including Speech Language Pathologists, Occupational Therapists, mental health professionals and Psychologists.

How does a provider get access?

Group licenses are purchased on an annual basis. Request a consultation to learn more about our volume discount pricing.

Is there a minimum purchase requirement?

There is no minimum purchase requirement. You can purchase one license if that meets the current needs of your team.

How much does this cost?

Pricing varies based on the type of license that meets your needs and the number of licenses that are purchased. Set up time for a consultation so that we can help find the right solution for your budget.

How do the assessments work on this platform?

Working with our publishing partners like Pearson and Riverside, we have digitally embedded the testing and instructional materials. Our approach meets the strict evaluation protocol and often makes it easier to observe the child’s behaviors. Reach out to us so that we can demo the assessment tool so that you can judge the tool for yourself.
